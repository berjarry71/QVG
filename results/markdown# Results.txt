markdown# Results

This folder will contain the numerical results of the QVG fixed-point algorithm:

- `Y_star_up.npy`      — Yukawa matrix Y*_u at the fixed point
- `Y_star_down.npy`    — Yukawa matrix Y*_d at the fixed point  
- `Y_star_lepton.npy`  — Yukawa matrix Y*_e at the fixed point
- `Y_star_neutrino.npy`— Yukawa matrix Y*_ν at the fixed point
- `rho_star.npy`       — Fixed-point distribution ρ*
- `convergence_8starts.csv` — Convergence data from 8 initial conditions
- `observables.csv`    — Predicted vs PDG 2024 values
